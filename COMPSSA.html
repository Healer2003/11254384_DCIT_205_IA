Dear Prospective student,
As you navigate the landscape of higher education,COMPSSA-UoG stands as your guiding star through the diverse phases of academic pursuit. Whether you are embarking on the exciting journey of a Barchelor's degree,delving into the depths of a Master's degree program or reaching the pinnacle of knowledge with a Ph.D., we are committed to supporting your intellectual ascent. For our Bachelor's candidates, the Computer Science Students Association of The University of Ghana(COMPSSA-UoG) will serve as a foundation offering mentorship, study resources, and engaging events to complement your undergraduate studies. As you unlock the mysteries of of Computer Science, our community stands united to ensure your success. If you are venturing into the realm of Master's program, we promise you a dynamic environment at COMPSSA, where advanced learning converges with collaborative endeavors. Our support extends to research opportunities, networking events and a community of peers dedicated to elevating the academic experience. To our esteemed Ph.D. candidates,COMPSSA recognizes the magnitude of your scholarly pursuit. That is why we provide a platform where groundbreaking research meets intellectual discourse, fostering an environment conducive to innovation and academic excellence. As you embark on these diverse academic journeys, COMPSSA remains committed to being your companion in every step. Welcome to our community. This is where educational exploration knows no bounds.

Events

Get ready for a jam-packed calendar of events tailored to enhance your academic journey. COMPSSA offers a range of exciting activities, from hackathons and coding competitions to enlightening guest lectures and networking events.
