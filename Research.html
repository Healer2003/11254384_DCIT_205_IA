Research At UG DCS

At our institution, education is not confined to traditional lectures; it's a dynamic expedition enriched with hands-on research activities that spark real-world impact and innovation. Guided by our distinguished faculty, you'll explore a spectrum of research fields, from cutting-edge advancements in wireless systems and augmented reality to an array of disciplines tailored to suit your unique interests. Whether your passion lies in artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, or human-computer interaction, our collaborative learning environment empowers you to delve into meaningful research alongside faculty mentors and fellow students. This personalized approach is designed to not only hone your skills in the field of Computer Science but also nurture your intellectual curiosity, fostering a comprehensive educational experience that extends far beyond the classroom.

Meet Dr. Ebenezer Owusu, a renowned expert in Computer Vision, whose groundbreaking research has pushed boundaries.

Dr. Ebenezer Owusu, Ph.D., heads our department, specializing in Computer Vision, Pattern Recognition, and Image Processing. His leadership fosters innovation and excellence in technology.

Joining forces with Mark Attah Mensah, a pioneer in augmented reality, you'll delve into the immersive world where virtual and real-life experiences converge.

Mark Attah Mensah,lecturer in Software Engineering, spearheads research in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, elevating our academic pursuits. His expertise propels innovation, fostering a dynamic learning environment.

As you embark on your academic journey with us, the research-rich landscape awaits, promising not just knowledge acquisition but an immersive experience where your contributions become part of the evolving tapestry of advancements in Computer Science
