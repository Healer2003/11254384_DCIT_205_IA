Welcome!

We're thrilled about your interest in our Computer Science Department's diverse degree programs. Embracing students from various backgrounds is central to our mission at the University of Ghana. We strongly believe that a diverse student body enhances the educational experience for everyone and contributes to advancing knowledge.Our commitment to fostering inclusivity goes beyond words. We offer a supportive and collaborative learning environment where you can engage in cutting-edge research, work with esteemed faculty, and contribute to a dynamic academic community. Diversity is not just a goal for us; it's a cornerstone of our programs. Join us at the forefront of computer science education, where your unique background and experiences are valued. Discover the exciting opportunities that await you in artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, and more. At the University of Ghana, we're shaping the future of computer science together. Welcome to a community dedicated to excellence, innovation, and diversity.

International Students

We warmly invite international students in search of a lively academic community to join us. Becoming a part of our diverse and inclusive environment opens doors to enriching experiences beyond traditional classrooms. Our dedication to global perspectives elevates the learning atmosphere, fostering meaningful cultural exchanges. As a member of our community, you'll not only gain access to a top-tier education but also immerse yourself in a new culture, actively contributing to the wealth of ideas that characterize our institution. Seize the chance to broaden your horizons and flourish in an international setting with us.

International students should visit the International Students Admissions website for more information.
