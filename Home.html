Our Programs

The University of Ghana's department of Computer Science is dedicated to equipping students for the dynamic realm of technology. Through our flexible curriculum, undergraduates can opt for a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science or pursue a minor in the same discipline. Our Master's degree offers graduates the opportunity to further enhance their expertise in the field.

We Value Curiosity and Gamechanging Ideas

Within the enclave of the School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences, the Computer Science Department orchestrates a curriculum meticlously crafted around the pillars of research and innovation. Guided by seasoned faculty and enriched with ample resources, students embark and a transformative educational odyssey, where they not only expand their knowledge but also carve out breakthroughs that resonate with the forefront of technological advancement.

Our Research

The CS Department is a hub for groundbreaking research and progressive education,advancing discoveries in AI,robotics, and Data Science.

Our Faculty

Our faculty members are dedicated to addressing some of the world's most formidable challenges through collaborative efforts with students and experienced leaders bringing expertise from various STEM fields.

Short Courses

Our comprehensive eight week certificate program welcomes senior high school graduates, tertiary students,recent school leavers and the general public to enroll and benefit from this course.

Web Development

With our comprehensive Web Development short course, students are equipped with essential skills in HTML,CSS,and JavaScript. The curriculum covers both front-end and back-end development, providing hands-on experienced in creating dynamic and responsive websites.

Computer Networking

Embark on a journey of connectivity and communication with our Computer Networking course, tailored for aspiring professionals in the field of network infrastructure and data transmission.

Mobile App Development

Unlock the potential of the mobile ecosystem with our Mobile App Development course, tailored for aspiring developers seeking to create innovative and user-friendly applictaions for iOS and Android platforms.

Cybersecurity & Ethical Hacking

The department also proudly presents a cutting-edge Cybersecurity and Ethical Hacking course designed to equip students with the skills needed to safeguard digital landscapes and navigate ethical challenges in cybersecurity domain.
