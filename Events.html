Events

The University Of Ghana Computer Science Department is packed with a lot of activities and events to keep the students entertained. All the way from seminars and competitions with other tech related departments on campus to our most anticipated annual code challenge-"The GreenByte Hackaton", we make sure students get away from the stress of sitting behind their laptops.

GreenByte Hackfest

Explore Innovation and sustainability at UG DCS.

At UG DCS, COMPSSA's annual tradition of "GreenByte Hackfest" continues to captivate the imagination of our tech-savvy community. This recurring event has become synonymous with the intersection of technology and environmental stewardship, offering a platform for sustainable tech innovation year after year.

Yearly Gathering Of Tech Enthusiasts

GreenByte Hackfest has evolved into a much anticipated yearly gathering bringing together students,faculty and industry professionals with a shared passion for leveraging technology to address environmental challenges. It has become a staple in the department's calendar, a testament to our commitment to sustainability.

Driving Innovation for a Sustainable Future

The event's core mission remains unwavering- to drive innovation for a more sustainable future. Each year, participants eagerly accept the challenge to develop technological solutions during the 24-hour coding marathon. This tradition has become a cornerstone of our commitment to using Computer Science as a force for positive change.

Building Lasting Connections

Beyond the code, this event fosters lasting connections among faculty members and members of the COMPSSA community. Participants not only collaborate with peers but also engage with mentors and industry experts. This tradition has cultivated a supportive community which extends beyond the event and has created networks that contribute to ongoing discussions on tech-driven sustainability.

Bytes That Matter Every Year

Our event is more than just a hackathon; it is a commitment to writing lines of code that matter every year, underscoring our dedication to fostering a culture where technology serves as a catalyst for positive environmental impact.

As we gear towards another year of GreenByte Hackfest,let us celebtrate this tradition,embracing the opportunity to showcase the innovative spirit of the Computer Science Department.
