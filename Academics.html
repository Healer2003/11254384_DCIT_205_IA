Department Of Computer Science

Academics

In the heart of the School Physical and Mathematical Sciences lies the Department of Computer Science, a center for innovative education and research at both undergraduate and graduate levels. Strong research groups exist in the fields of Artificial Intelligence,Robotics,Virtual Reality, Data Science and many. The Department holds and maintains ties with researchers with computational interests in other departments in the University.

Degree Programs

The Department of Computer Science at the University of Ghana operates under the School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences (SPMS). Within this academic framework, the department provides academic programs leading to the degrees of Bachelor of Science, Master of Science, and Doctor of Philosophy. Additionally, the department offers short courses that cater to individuals at all levels of education.
